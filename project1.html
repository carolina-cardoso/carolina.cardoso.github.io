Social Media Video

Role: Video production and post-production Produced at Euronews

I produced this vertical social media video for Smart Regions , an Euronews magazine sponsored by the European Commission (Directorate-General for Regional and Urban Policy).

Everything in this video was done by me:

  • Conceptualisation
  • Interview with an expert for fact-checked information
  • Scriptwriting and storyboard
  • On-camera presenting
  • Setting up lights, camera tripods, and microphones
  • Mobile-first video editing, animations, and post-production, including colour and sound correction with Adobe Premiere Pro
  • Thumbnail with Canva
